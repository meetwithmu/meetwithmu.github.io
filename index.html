Mu

Meeting up has never been easier.

With Mu, coordinating plans with your friends is simple.

First, join one of our rooms with your friends. Once you're all there, you can:

  • See where they are at a glance
  • See how far they're willing to travel
  • Suggest places to meet
  • Vote on where you want to go
  • Real-time updates to make it easier to reach a consensus

By visualizing how each person's travel time overlaps, we make it easy to see locations that are most convenient for everyone.
